Why We Choose the Name Margasatvva?

You might be wondering, why on earth did we pick the name Margasatvva?

Well, Margasatvva actually comes from the word 'MARGASATWA', which means a home for all forms of undomesticated animals living freely in the wild. Think of it as the untamed wilderness where creatures roam free, unburdened by human intervention.

We chose this name because it perfectly captures our spirit: wild, free, and true to nature. It's a tribute to the raw beauty and untamed essence of the natural world, and that's exactly what we aim to embody.
